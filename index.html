Hunger Games

School of survival

The purpose of our travels is to give people the opportunity to overcome their fears, develop willpower, and become better. In addition to testing yourself and overcoming difficulties, you will be able to provide humanitarian assistance to remote tribes and villages that we will meet along the way. Part of the funds paid for the tour will be used for humanitarian assistance. Medicines, clothing, educational supplies are in short supply for people cut off from civilization. We will deliver the things they need and make this world a little better with the help of our journey. More
